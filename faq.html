FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

When is ROYALMUN

11-14 MAY 2018

 What is dress code for ROYALMUN

The delegates are expected to wear formal during the Conference including the opening ceremony. Business attire shall be worn by the female participants and suits, jackets and tie/bowties would be appropriate for male participants. Social events do not have a dress code. Delegates may wear casual during the events. Female delegates must have appropriate bottoms and tops that conforms to formal debate environment.

 Where and when is ROYALMUN 2018 going to be held?

ROYALMUN 2018 will take place at Rotary 100. Yıl Anadolu Lisesi.

 Can I represent my own country?

Individual delegates are not allowed to represent their own countries and delegations aren’t allowed to represent countries of their institutions.

 Are position papers required from every delegate?

Yes, delegates are expected to send position papers to the committee board.

 What does the conference fee cover?

It includes the lunches, conference materials, hot beverages and snacks during the conference.

 How many delegates can apply as a delegation?

There should be at least 4 and maximum 15 delegates to form a delegation. In case there are less than four delegates from the same institution/school, we recommend the delegates to apply individually. If there are more than 15 delegates from one institution/school, delegates may form two different delegations.
